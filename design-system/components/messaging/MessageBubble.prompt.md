A plain text message bubble for the digest/reply demo. Incoming bubbles are dark on the left; the operator's replies are violet on the right.

```jsx
<MessageBubble from="them" emoji="🗓" time="7:42 AM">
  Tue. 4 items, 1 needs you. M51: ship the diligence memo before noon.
</MessageBubble>
<MessageBubble from="me" time="7:43 AM">done M51</MessageBubble>
```

Keep content plain text (no markdown, no bullets) to match the iMessage format contract.
