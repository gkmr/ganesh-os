The bracket source tag that leads every outbound agent message, per the house-style format contract.

```jsx
<ChannelTag channel="gmail" /> 4 items, 1 needs you.
<ChannelTag channel="voice" /> V3: callback from the GP office.
```

Channels: gmail/email (cyan), slack (violet), imessage/sms (teal), whatsapp (green), voice (orange), intake (amber). Always lowercase inside brackets.
